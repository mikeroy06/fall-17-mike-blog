Have you ever heard of the band Pink Floyd? You haven't? Well, take a seat.


Pink Floyd is one of the most famous bands of all time and my all-time favorite. They originated in the 1960's. Lead singer Syd Barrett left the band shortly after their first album Piper at the Gates of Dawn and was replaced by David Gilmour. As they progressed, bassist Roger Waters began to take a more leading role. His final album with the band was entitled The Final Cut. Afterwards, recently ousted keyboardist Rick Wright reentered the band as they continued onward with their album Momentary Lapse of Reason.


They recorded one more studio album entitled The Division Bell. All 4 members came back together for a 30 minute set for Live Aid in London in 2005.


Sadly, original vocalist Syd Barrett passed away in 2006. Two years later keyboardist Rick Wright passed away as well.


I hope you enjoyed this musical journey through the illustrious career of Pink Floyd. I know you did. Otherwise, you fine people wouldn't be reading my blog.
